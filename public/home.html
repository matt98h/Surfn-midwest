What is Surf'n Midwest?

The midwest is less Beach Boys and more Soy Boys, however for a brave few who are willing to suffer through the cold winters, can find some of the best surfing in the world. This was news to us, and is why we decided to make this application. Surf'n Midwest is the midwests premiere community based surfing application that allows users to search the most popular midwest surfing spots and see images, how rad the place is and comments from other users! We hope to see you posting!
